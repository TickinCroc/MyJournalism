New Study Reveals Alarming Decline in Global Bee Population

by Aram Jackie Jang

A recent study conducted by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) has highlighted a concerning decline in the global bee population. The report, published in the Journal of Environmental Science, indicates that a combination of factors, including habitat loss, pesticide use, and climate change, has led to a 30% decrease in been colonies worldwide over the past decade.

Researchers emphasize the critical role of bees in pollination and ecosystem stability, calling for urgent measures to protect and preserve these vital pollinators.
